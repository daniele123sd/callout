Privacy Policy

Effective: 29 June 2026

Information we collect

Callout may collect account details you provide, profile information, content you publish, votes, messages, guild activity, authentication identifiers, and basic technical logs needed to operate and secure the service.

How information is used

Information is used to provide accounts and social features, personalize the experience, prevent abuse, maintain security, communicate service updates, and understand product performance.

Analytics and advertising

With the choices provided through our consent controls, Callout may use Google Analytics to measure visits, page views, acquisition channels, and product performance, and Google AdSense to deliver and measure advertising. Google and its partners may use cookies or similar technologies for these purposes. Reporting shown to the Callout administrator is aggregated and does not expose account passwords or private messages.

Authentication and cookies

Authentication uses secure, HTTP-only cookies. Google sign-in may share your Google account identifier, email address, display name, and avatar with Callout after you approve access.

Sharing and retention

We do not sell personal information. Information may be disclosed to service providers that help operate Callout, including hosting, authentication, analytics, and advertising providers, when required by law, or to protect users and the service. Records are retained only as long as reasonably necessary for these purposes.

Your choices

You may update your profile and preferences in Settings and use the available consent controls for analytics and advertising. You may request access, correction, or deletion of account information by contacting the project operator.

Children

Callout is not intended for children under 13. Accounts may only be created by people who confirm they are at least 13 years old.

Security and contact

Reasonable safeguards are used, but no online service can guarantee absolute security. Do not share sensitive personal information. Questions may be directed to the project operator through the official support channel.
